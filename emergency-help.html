Emergency Help

Effective date: May 1, 2026

MixAware is not for emergencies. If you or someone else may be experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.

Call emergency services now for

  • Suspected overdose or poisoning
  • Chest pain
  • Trouble breathing
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Seizure
  • Severe bleeding
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Extreme confusion
  • Suicidal intent or risk of self-harm
  • Rapidly worsening symptoms

Poison Control

For poisoning concerns in the United States, contact Poison Control:

1-800-222-1222

Important

Do not use MixAware to decide what to do in an emergency. MixAware does not replace emergency medical care, poison control, a doctor, pharmacist, or licensed clinician.
